About Us

We believe in creating joy through every scoop. Our ice creams are made with love, passion, and the finest ingredients.

Founded in 2020, Armida Ice Cream started as a small family business with a big dream — to spread happiness through delightful frozen treats. Inspired by childhood memories and endless experiments in our cozy kitchen, we’ve crafted flavors that not only taste amazing but also bring smiles to faces of all ages. 🍦💕

From fruity favorites to chocolatey indulgences, each scoop tells a story of sweetness and warmth. Join us in our journey to make the world a little more magical, one cone at a time. ✨🍓
